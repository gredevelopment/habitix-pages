Terms of Use Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how GRE Development LTD (“GRE Development,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) handles information when you use Habitix, our iPhone/iPad app, Apple Watch app, widgets, and related services (collectively, the “App”).

Habitix is designed to keep your habit data primarily on your devices. We do not require you to create an account, and we do not operate a Habitix cloud account that stores your habits on our servers.

1. Who we are

Habitix is owned and operated by GRE Development LTD, a private limited company registered in England and Wales (Company Number 13031797), with its registered office at 24-26 Arcadia Avenue, Fin009, London, United Kingdom, N3 2JU. You can reach us at info@gredev.io or via gredev.io.

2. Who this policy applies to

This policy applies to people who download, install, or use Habitix on Apple platforms. If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use the App.

3. Information we process

Depending on how you use Habitix, the App may process:

  • Habit and progress data you enter — habit names, descriptions, icons, colors, schedules, reminder preferences, completion logs, streaks, challenges, and related settings.
  • Device and system permissions you grant — for example, permission to send local notifications so Habitix can remind you about habits.
  • Purchase and subscription status — processed by Apple through StoreKit / App Store billing when you start a trial, subscribe, restore purchases, or manage subscriptions.
  • On-device preferences — such as onboarding answers, display preferences (for example grid style or layout), and similar settings stored locally.

Habitix does not ask for your email address, phone number, or social-network login to use the core App. If you contact us by email, we will receive whatever information you include in that message.

4. Where your data lives

  • On your Apple devices. Habit and completion data are stored locally using Apple frameworks (including SwiftData / on-device storage).
  • App Group storage on your devices. Habitix may share a limited snapshot of habit progress with Habitix widgets and, if installed, the Habitix Apple Watch app via Apple’s App Group and Watch Connectivity mechanisms. This sharing stays within your Apple devices and Apple system services — it is not a Habitix-operated cloud database.
  • Apple services. Purchases, subscriptions, restorations, and related receipts are handled by Apple. We receive entitlement information needed to unlock paid features; we do not receive your full payment card details.

If you use Apple features such as iCloud Backup for your device, Apple may include App data in backups according to your Apple ID and device settings. That processing is governed by Apple’s privacy policy and your Apple settings.

5. How we use information

We use the information described above to:

  • Provide Habitix features (tracking, reminders, streaks, challenges, widgets, Watch companion, insights).
  • Process subscriptions and restore purchases through Apple.
  • Improve reliability and fix bugs (for example, crash diagnostics if you choose to share them with Apple/developers).
  • Respond to support requests you send us.
  • Comply with law and enforce our Terms of Use.

We do not sell your personal information. We do not use your habit history to build advertising profiles for third-party ads inside Habitix.

6. Notifications

If you enable reminders, Habitix schedules local notifications on your device. Notification content may include habit names or descriptions you provided. You can disable notifications in iOS Settings or inside Habitix. Deleting or deactivating a habit cancels that habit’s pending Habitix reminder schedules.

7. Analytics and tracking

Habitix’s in-app “Insights” screens summarize your own habit data on device. As of the effective date above, Habitix does not integrate third-party advertising SDKs or third-party behavioral advertising trackers.

Apple may provide aggregated, privacy-preserving developer analytics (for example App Store analytics or crash reports) according to your device and App Store settings.

8. Sharing of information

We share information only in these limited ways:

  • With Apple — as required to deliver App Store distribution, StoreKit purchases, notifications, widgets, and Watch sync.
  • With service providers — only if we later use providers that process data on our behalf under contract (for example email support tooling). We will update this policy if that changes in a material way.
  • For legal reasons — if required by law, regulation, legal process, or to protect rights, safety, and security.
  • Business transfers — if GRE Development LTD is involved in a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, information may transfer as part of that transaction subject to this policy or successor notice.

9. Data retention and deletion

Because habit data is stored on your devices, deleting the Habitix app (and related Watch app / widgets data, as applicable) generally removes that local Habitix data from the device, subject to any device backups you keep.

You can also delete individual habits inside the App, which removes that habit’s history in Habitix and cancels its pending reminders.

Subscription records remain with Apple according to Apple’s retention practices. Managing or canceling a subscription is done through your Apple ID subscription settings.

10. Children’s privacy

Habitix is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your jurisdiction), and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information to us, contact us and we will take appropriate steps.

11. International users

Habitix is distributed through the Apple App Store. Processing happens primarily on your devices and through Apple’s infrastructure. If you use Habitix outside your home country, your information may be processed in the locations where you and Apple operate.

12. Your privacy choices

  • Control notification permissions in iOS Settings.
  • Delete habits or uninstall Habitix to remove on-device Habitix data (subject to backups).
  • Manage subscriptions and payment methods in Apple ID → Subscriptions.
  • Review Apple’s privacy controls for analytics, tracking, and App Store preferences on your device.

13. Changes to this policy
